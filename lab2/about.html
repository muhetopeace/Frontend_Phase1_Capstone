About Book Explorer

Book Explorer is a responsive web app that helps users discover, search, and save their favorite books. It uses the Open Library API and allows you to manage your favorite books dynamically.

Mission

Our mission is to make book discovery easy, enjoyable, and personalized. We want to encourage reading by providing an interactive and organized way to explore books.

How to Use

- Browse the popular books on the home page.
- Click "Add to Favorites" to save a book.
- Go to the Favorites page to view, remove, or clear your favorite books.
- Use the search bar (future enhancement) to find specific books easily.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS (Tailwind), JavaScript, localStorage for persistence.

Future Plans

We plan to include book categories, personalized recommendations, and user reviews to enhance the reading experience.
